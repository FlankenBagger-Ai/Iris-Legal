What IRIS is

IRIS is a personal content-automation tool built and operated by the streamer FlankenBagger. It edits gameplay recordings and publishes the finished videos to FlankenBagger's own social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, X).

Who can use it

IRIS is a single-user application. It is not offered to the public, has no user registration, and posts only to accounts owned by its operator. There are no third-party users and no user-generated content from anyone else.

Content

All content IRIS publishes is FlankenBagger's own gameplay footage and commentary. Publishing to each platform is subject to that platform's own terms, which the operator accepts directly on those platforms.

IRIS is provided as-is for the operator's own use, with no warranties and no service commitments to anyone else.
