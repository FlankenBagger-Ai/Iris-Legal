IRIS — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

The short version

IRIS is a personal, single-user tool. It collects no data from anyone other than its own operator, FlankenBagger.

What it stores

IRIS stores OAuth tokens for the operator's own social media accounts. These are kept locally on the operator's personal computer, are used solely to upload the operator's own videos to the operator's own accounts, and are never shared, sold, or transmitted anywhere except to the respective platform's official API.

What it doesn't do

IRIS has no users besides its operator, no analytics, no tracking, no cookies, no advertising, and no collection of anyone else's personal information. Data received from platform APIs (such as upload confirmations) is used only to display posting status to the operator.

Data deletion

All stored credentials can be deleted at any time by the operator by disconnecting the account in IRIS, which removes the stored token.

Contact

Reach FlankenBagger via twitch.tv/flankenbagger.
